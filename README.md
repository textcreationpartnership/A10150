    _ The effect of Wyates letter to sir Robert Southwell Shreif of Kent.

    _ The style of wyates proclamation. A proclamation agreed vnto by Thomas Wyat, George Harper, Henrye Isleye,Wyates fals• presumption of the best of the shyre. knightes, and by diuers of the best of the shyre, sent vnto the commons of the same.

    _ The Shreifes answere to the messenger that brought Wyates letter.

    _ Certayne wordes proceding from Wyat at hys arraynem••t.
